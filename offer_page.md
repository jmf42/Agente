# AI Visibility Starter Audit

## One-line Offer

Find out whether AI assistants can access, understand, and accurately describe your business website.

## Who It Is For

This is for small businesses, agencies, consultants, SaaS tools, ecommerce stores, and creators who already have a website and want to be easier for AI systems to understand.

## The Problem

People increasingly ask AI assistants for recommendations, comparisons, and explanations. If your site is hard to parse, missing structured facts, blocking crawlers, or unclear about what you do, AI systems may ignore you or describe you badly.

This audit does not promise rankings. It gives you a practical readiness report and the first files/content blocks needed to make your website clearer to AI systems and human visitors.

- Structured data check for JSON-LD and FAQ schema.
