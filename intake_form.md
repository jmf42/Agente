# Intake Form

Ask the buyer for only what is needed to complete the starter audit.

## Required

1. Website URL.
2. Business name.
3. Business category.
4. Main city, region, or market served.
5. Top 3 services or products.
6. Ideal customer.
7. One sentence describing why customers choose you.
8. Top competitors, if known.
9. Any pages you especially want AI assistants to understand.

## Optional

1. Google Business Profile URL.
2. Yelp, Clutch, G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, or other review/profile URLs.
3. Existing SEO agency or web developer contact, if implementation is wanted later.

## Do Not Request

- Passwords.
- PayPal login.
- Bank details.
- CMS credentials.
- API keys.
- Private customer data.
